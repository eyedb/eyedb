Welcome to the EYEDB Browser Connection Window


EYEDB is an OODBMS developped at Sysra Informatique in collaboration with Infobiogen, the ANVAR and the Conseil Regional d'Ile de France.

EYEDB is a very efficient and reliable OODBMS, providing all standard OODBMS features.

You can find information on EYEDB at http://www.sysra.com/eyedb.
